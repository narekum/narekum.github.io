Reaching us

 

The beautiful campus of Jaypee University of Information Technology is situated 3 kilometers from Waknaghat on the National Highway NH-22 that connects Chandigarh to Shimla via Kalka. Nearest cities are Shimla and Solan, both of which are well connected to Waknaghat with Bus and Taxi services. There is a free shuttle service available in the morning and evening connecting Waknaghat to the campus.
